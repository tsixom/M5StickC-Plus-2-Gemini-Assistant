M5StickC Plus 2 Gemini Assistant

Instructions:
1. Connect your M5StickC Plus 2 via USB to your computer.
2. Use Chrome or Edge browser (desktop).
3. Click the "Install" button above and select your device.
4. Wait for flashing to finish. Device will reboot automatically.

(If you see a permission pop-up, grant access to the COM/serial device. Files must be published via GitHub Pages; if you get a 404, check that your files are in the correct folder and branch.)
