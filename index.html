⚡ M5StickC Plus 2 — Gemini Assistant

Voice assistant with on‑device UI: record → speech‑to‑text → Gemini → scrollable replies, plus clock, stopwatch, brightness, and stealth. Support

Install Firmware

Use a Chromium‑based browser with Web Serial/WebUSB over HTTPS.
If the device isn’t detected, try a different USB cable/port and close any serial monitor apps.

What You Get

One‑Tap Voice → AI

Press A to record 5s, auto upload to STT, send transcript to Gemini, and view a scrollable AI reply.

Conversation Context

Maintains short chat history, trims after ~10 convos, and shows count as C:X in the status bar.

Stealth & Inactivity

Shake to switch to clock stealth mode, or auto‑stealth after 30s inactivity.

Clock + Stopwatch

Digital clock with analog seconds ring, manual time/date setup, full stopwatch (start/pause/reset).

Power Smart

6‑level brightness, battery meter/voltage, auto sleep, dynamic brightness lower on low battery.

Wi‑Fi Portal

Config portal to save Gemini API key and STT URL; long‑press B(6s) to reset Wi‑Fi+keys.

How It Works

  • Short press A to RECORD for 5s — on‑screen indicator shows status.
  • Audio is sent to your STT server as WAV; transcript is displayed (“You Said”).
  • Transcript is sent to Gemini; response appears as a scrollable view (“AI Response”).
  • Conversation history is retained for context and trimmed automatically over time.

Controls

  • A short: start 5s recording; long while viewing: scroll up.
  • C short: scroll down in views.
  • B short: in clock mode, advance brightness level; in AI mode, prompt to clear chat.
  • B long(1.5s): enter clock time/date settings.
  • B very long(6s): wipe Wi‑Fi+keys (safe reset).
  • A+B or A+C hold: return from clock stealth to AI mode.
  • Shake: emergency switch to stealth clock at any time.

Status Indicators

Recording — Red dot on screen
AI Thinking — Banner shown
