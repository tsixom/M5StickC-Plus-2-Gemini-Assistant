Use a Chromium-based browser with Web Serial/WebUSB over HTTPS.

Quick Steps

Connect your M5StickC Plus 2 to the computer via USB.
Click “Connect” above and select your device from the list.

Wait for flashing to complete — the device will reboot automatically.
