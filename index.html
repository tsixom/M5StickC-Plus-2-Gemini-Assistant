⚡ M5StickC Plus 2 — Gemini Assistant Flasher

Flash the latest build to your M5StickC Plus 2 directly from the browser.

Install Firmware

Requires a Chromium-based browser with Web Serial/WebUSB support over HTTPS.
Tip: If the device isn’t detected, try a different USB cable/port and close any serial monitor apps.

Quick Steps

Connect your M5StickC Plus 2 to the computer via USB.
Click “Connect” above and select your device from the list.
Choose “M5StickC Plus 2 Gemini Assistant” and click Install.
Wait for flashing to complete — the device will reboot automatically.
